What is a Virus?

Just like humans can get be affected by illneses and viruses, computers can

also be affected. A computer virus is malicious software or malware that when

operating on a computer, can damage or destroy the system software. Eventually

even sometimes making the computer unusable. There are multiple ways to get a virus

on a computer. A few of the ways a virus spreads is by:

  • Email and text message attachments
  • Internet file downloads
  • Social media scam links
