Trojan Horse

There are a variety of ways a trojan horse virus can sneek into your computer without detection. However, most tend to do the same type of harms when getting hold of your computer.

A trojan is malware that is posed to be legitimate software to access the private information in your computer. Once the malware gets hold of the computer it can:

  • Disrupt the performance of computers or computer networks
  • Delete Data
  • Block Data
  • Modify Data

The difference between this virus and other viruses, is that it does not have the capability to spread to other computers
